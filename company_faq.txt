NimbusCart Customer Support FAQ
================================
Welcome to NimbusCart, your one-stop online marketplace for electronics, home
goods, and lifestyle products. This document answers the most common
questions our customers ask. Please read the relevant section carefully
before contacting support.

1. REFUND POLICY
-----------------
NimbusCart offers a 30-day return window from the date of delivery for most
products. To be eligible for a refund, items must be unused, in their
original packaging, and accompanied by the original invoice.

Refund timelines depend on the payment method used at checkout:
- Credit or debit card payments are refunded within 5-7 business days after
  the returned item passes quality inspection at our warehouse.
- UPI and net banking refunds are processed within 3-5 business days.
- Wallet payments (NimbusCart Wallet) are refunded instantly upon approval.
- Cash on Delivery (COD) orders are refunded via bank transfer within 7-10
  business days, since we need to collect the customer's bank details first.

Certain categories are non-refundable, including innerwear, perishable
goods, customized products, and digital gift cards. Products marked
"Final Sale" on the product page are also excluded from the refund policy.

If a product arrives damaged or defective, customers can request a full
refund or a free replacement within 48 hours of delivery, no questions
asked. Please attach photos of the damaged item when raising the request
so our quality team can process it faster.

Refund requests can be raised from the "My Orders" section of the app or
website by selecting the order and clicking "Request Refund."

2. DELIVERY TIMELINES
----------------------
Standard delivery timelines vary by location:
- Metro cities (Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Chennai, Kolkata,
  Pune): 2-3 business days.
- Tier 2 cities: 4-5 business days.
- Remote or rural areas: 6-8 business days.

Express delivery is available for select pin codes and select products,
offering next-day delivery for an additional fee. Express eligibility is
shown on the product page before checkout.

Order tracking is available in real time through the "Track Order" section
once the order has been shipped. Customers will also receive SMS and email
updates at each stage: order confirmed, shipped, out for delivery, and
delivered.

Delays may occur during festive seasons, extreme weather conditions, or due
to regional logistics disruptions. In such cases, NimbusCart will notify
affected customers proactively via email and app notification.

3. MEMBERSHIP BENEFITS
------------------------
NimbusCart offers three membership tiers: Silver, Gold, and Platinum.
Membership is free to join but tiers are upgraded automatically based on
annual spend, or customers can subscribe directly to NimbusCart Plus for
instant tier upgrades.

Silver Tier (default for all customers):
- Standard shipping rates apply.
- Access to seasonal sales.
- Standard customer support during business hours.

Gold Tier (unlocked after annual spend of INR 25,000 or NimbusCart Plus
subscription):
- Free standard shipping on all orders.
- Early access to sales, 24 hours before Silver members.
- Priority customer support with reduced wait times.
- 5% cashback on NimbusCart Wallet for every purchase.

Platinum Tier (unlocked after annual spend of INR 75,000 or NimbusCart Plus
Premium subscription):
- Free express shipping on all eligible orders.
- Early access to sales, 48 hours before Silver members.
- Dedicated premium customer support with a personal support agent and
  average response time under 10 minutes.
- 10% cashback on NimbusCart Wallet for every purchase.
- Extended return window of 45 days instead of the standard 30 days.
- Complimentary extended warranty on electronics purchases above INR 5,000.

Membership tier is reviewed and updated quarterly based on rolling
12-month spend.

4. ACCOUNT CANCELLATION
--------------------------
Customers can cancel or deactivate their NimbusCart account at any time by
going to Settings > Account > Deactivate Account. Deactivation is
temporary and can be reversed by simply logging back in within 90 days.

For permanent account deletion, customers must submit a request through
the "Delete My Account" option, which requires email confirmation. Once
confirmed, all personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days in
accordance with our data retention policy, except for information we are
legally required to retain for tax and accounting purposes (such as
invoices), which is retained for 7 years as mandated by law.

Before cancellation, customers should ensure there are no pending orders,
active refund requests, or outstanding NimbusCart Wallet balances, as
these cannot be recovered after account deletion. Any unused NimbusCart
Plus subscription time is forfeited upon cancellation and is not eligible
for a prorated refund.

Accounts with active Platinum or Gold membership benefits, including
pending cashback, must claim rewards before initiating deletion, as reward
balances cannot be transferred or reissued after account closure.

5. SUPPORT CONTACT HOURS
---------------------------
General customer support is available through chat, email, and phone
during the following hours:
- Monday to Saturday: 9:00 AM to 9:00 PM (local time)
- Sunday: 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM (local time)
- Support is closed on national public holidays, with a reduced-hours team
  handling urgent issues only.

Email support (support@nimbuscart.example) typically responds within 24
hours on business days.

Live chat is the fastest way to reach us and is available directly within
the app and website during support hours.

Phone support is available for order-related emergencies and payment
disputes only, and is prioritized for Gold and Platinum members.

6. PREMIUM CUSTOMER SUPPORT RULES
-------------------------------------
Premium customer support is exclusively available to Gold and Platinum
tier members and is designed to provide faster, more personalized
assistance.

Gold members receive:
- Priority placement in the support queue, ahead of Silver members.
- Access to live chat and phone support during all support hours.
- Average first-response time of under 30 minutes.

Platinum members receive:
- A dedicated premium support line with 24/7 availability, including
  holidays.
- A named personal support agent assigned to handle recurring issues.
- Average first-response time of under 10 minutes.
- Escalation privileges, meaning any unresolved issue is automatically
  escalated to a senior support specialist after 2 hours.
- Proactive order monitoring, where the support team reaches out
  automatically if a Platinum member's order is delayed or at risk of
  delay, rather than waiting for the customer to ask.

Silver members can access premium support features temporarily by
purchasing a one-time "Priority Support Pass," valid for 30 days from
purchase.

For any questions not covered in this document, please reach out to our
support team through the channels listed above.
